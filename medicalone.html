SaaS Solution for Medical Inventory Management

PROJECT SUMMARY

MedicalOne helps healthcare providers manage inventory with ease.

Many clinics and hospitals struggle with inefficient inventory tracking, leading to stockouts, overstocking, and waste. To address this, I developed a user-friendly, cloud-based SaaS solution that enables healthcare administrators to easily manage inventory, track medical supplies in real-time, and streamline procurement. Through user research and testing, MedicalOne ensures efficient resource management, improving operational workflows and patient care quality.

Transforming Medical Management

Problem statement:

Clinics and hospitals struggle with operational inefficiencies due to the absence of an integrated medical inventory management system, leading to challenges in tracking, restocking, and optimizing resources.

Identifying the potential users

We began by categorizing users into groups to understand their needs and identify key features for each group as well as what kind of access each group would have.

Sitemap and wireframes

After analyzing data collected by the UX research team, we began designing wireframes for the pages. The first step was determining which pages were essential for the MVP. We created three different Sitemaps based on the three types of users we had.

Branding and Design System

Developing a strong visual identity for the MedicalOne brand became the next key step. The platform needed to convey trust, professionalism, and reliability while maintaining a modern and accessible feel. After exploring various concepts, I finalized a logo, typography, color palette, and iconography that reflected these core values.
